## 1. Why do you think Focus Bear was created?

I believe that Focus Bear is designed to help those who are severely affected by ADHD regain the ability to maintain a regular lifestyle. According to the founder Jeremy's story on the official website, our company was born out of his efforts to bring his life back to normal.

## 2. What problem is Focus Bear solving?

Focusbear has addressed the issue for people with ADHD who struggle to concentrate on tasks that require productivity. Because gentle reminders are easily overlooked by people with ADHD, Focus Bear can cancel the screen-blocking mode by forcibly taking control of the screen.

## 3. Why do you think this mission is important?

Our work can effectively help people with ADHD return to being efficient, punctual and calm. Our app can directly and effectively help people enhance their cognitive abilities and reduce the troubles caused by ADHD.
